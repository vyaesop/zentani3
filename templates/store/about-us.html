Brand signal

Zentanee is building a faster fashion storefront for shoppers in Addis who still want the experience to feel styled.

We care about two things at the same time: the pieces need to feel distinct, and the buying flow needs to stay smooth on mobile. That means less clutter, better fit guidance, and support that actually helps instead of slowing the shopper down.

Based in Addis Ababa Mobile-first storefront Style + support
What we curate

Fashion with a sharper point of view.

Zentanee focuses on clothing, handbags, perfumes, and accessories that feel current without making the storefront read like a warehouse of random listings.

How we sell

Fast browse, clear fit, easy ordering.

The site is designed so shoppers can scan quickly, confirm size where needed, move to cart, and finish delivery details without getting lost in generic ecommerce clutter.

How we support

Real help stays close to the flow.

If a shopper needs help on fit, delivery, or availability, support is available through phone, Instagram, and Telegram without breaking the whole shopping rhythm.

Why the brand feels different

We are moving away from “just another ecommerce theme” and toward a store that feels curated, fast, and memorable.

The goal is not only to show products. The goal is to make the whole path from discovery to order feel more intentional: clearer product pages, better mobile scanning, and support that feels like a premium assist.

Core values
  • Curate pieces that feel current and distinct.
  • Keep the buying flow fast enough for mobile-first shoppers.
  • Make support and delivery feel dependable, not improvised.
