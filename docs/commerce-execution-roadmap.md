# Zentanee Commerce Execution Roadmap

## Goal
Turn Zentanee from a clean catalog into a stronger shopping system by borrowing the right mechanics from Shein and Grailed without inheriting their clutter.

## Positioning
- Copy from Shein:
  - faster discovery
  - stronger conversion cues
  - better browse/search controls
  - reviews and saved-shopping loops
- Copy from Grailed:
  - stronger trust language
  - richer product metadata
  - better post-purchase visibility
  - more disciplined item detail
- Avoid:
  - marketplace-only mechanics that do not fit a single-store brand
  - hyper-noisy promotional clutter

## P1
Status: Completed

### 1. Browse/Search Upgrade
- Add real sort controls across shop-all, search, category, and brand pages.
- Add visible filtering for:
  - price
  - availability
  - collections
  - brands
  - sizes
- Preserve filter state through pagination.
- Show active filter chips and a reset action.

### 2. PDP Trust Layer
- Add clearer delivery, returns, and fit guidance.
- Add richer product specs and stock messaging.
- Strengthen the area around size selection and add-to-cart.

### 3. Data Foundations
- Expand product data beyond title, description, and price.
- Move toward structured variants and stock by size.
- Add fields for measurements, material, color, and condition/trust notes where relevant.
